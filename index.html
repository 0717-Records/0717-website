Gang Violins

Ambient / cinematic electronic duo from Aotearoa New Zealand.

Sora Shima

Instrumental post-rock from New Zealand. Inspired by the majestic floating worlds often depicted in Japanese anime, Sora Shima ('Sky - Island') fuse elements of heavy indie, cinematic ambient and atmospheric electronic into something dynamic, immersive and entirely their own.

Shepherds Of Cassini

Mikey Videotape

Mikey Videotape’s musical journey is a testament to his unwavering dedication to pushing artistic boundaries. With a fusion of progressive rock, pop sensibilities, and a distinctive 80s-inspired aesthetic, Mikey Videotape invites you to embark on a mesmerising musical adventure that transcends time and genre.
